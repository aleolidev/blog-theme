## Main brands and alternatives

In our comparison, we have included only the best Chromebooks on the market. Likewise, we have taken into account the opinions and reviews of users to offer you the best purchase options. Why? Discover them below!

### Acer Nitro 5

<FloatingImage product={ props.products[0] } float={ "left" } />

The <a href={`/${props.lang}`} aria-invalid="true">Acer Nitro 5</a> is a gaming laptop with a 15.6-inch screen, an eight-core Intel Core i5-9300H processor, an NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 4GB GDDR5 graphics card and 16 GB RAM. With this laptop, you can play almost any current game and, in addition, it is much cheaper than other more powerful gaming laptops.

This laptop has a 128 GB SSD hard drive and a 1 TB HDD solid state drive, so you can save all your movies and series without a problem. In addition, it has a next-generation screen with a resolution of Full HD and a refresh rate of 144 Hz so you can enjoy a really sharp and vivid image.

### Acer Aspire 5 A515-46-R3UB


<FloatingImage product={ props.products[3] } float={ "right" } />

The <a href={`/${props.lang}`} aria-invalid="true">Acer Aspire 5 A515-46-R3UB</a> is a laptop with a 15.6″ Full HD screen, an AMD Ryzen 3 3350U processor and 4 GB of RAM. In addition, it has a 128 GB SSD drive to store all your files and games. The Acer Aspire 5 is equipped with WiFi 6 connection and an integrated fingerprint reader.

This model includes the Windows Home operating system in S mode, a secure system that protects the computer against malware and phishing. It also includes the Amazon Alexa voice assistant, which allows you to interact with the equipment with voice commands. The performance of the AMD Ryzen 3 3350U is up to 3.5 GHz. On the other hand, its battery lasts up to 7 hours on a full charge.

*   Pros: User reviews highlight how easy it is to install the Linux operating system.

*   Cons: User reviews indicate that the keyboard is not very comfortable.

## Samsung Chromebook 4


<FloatingImage product={ props.products[1] } float={ "left" } />

The <a href={`/${props.lang}`} aria-invalid="true">Samsung Chromebook 4</a> is a very popular _Chromebook_ notebook among users. It features an Intel Celeron N4000 processor, 4GB of RAM, and 32GB of eMMC storage.

This model has an 11.6-inch screen and a built-in HD camera. In addition, it includes Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and USB-C connections. It is a very light and compact device, so it is ideal to carry from one place to another.

In user reviews and comparisons, its battery stands out, which lasts more than 12 hours. It is also highlighted that it is easy to use thanks to the intuitive user interface of Chrome OS.

### Apple MacBook Air 2020

The <a href={`/${props.lang}`} aria-invalid="true">Apple MacBook Air 2020</a> is a laptop with a very elegant and light design. It weighs only 1.1 kg and has a backlit keyboard. Its 13.3-inch Retina display offers a unique visual experience thanks to a resolution of 2256 x 1440 pixels.

<FloatingImage product={ props.products[2] } float={ "right" } />

It features an Apple M1 chip with an 8-core CPU and up to 8GB RAM. Also, its SSD storage can reach 256GB.

On the other hand, its battery lasts up to 18 hours and it has USB-C and Thunderbolt 3 connections. You can connect to the Internet with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. In addition, it includes a backlit keyboard and a touch screen. The Apple MacBook Air 2020 is, therefore, a very complete laptop with a great value for money.
